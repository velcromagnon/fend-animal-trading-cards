Timberwolf

Minnesota has more than twice the number of wolves than any state apart from Alaska. However, in Yellowstone, Wolves have changed an entire ecosystem.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Average Lifespan: 5-6 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Canada, Alaska, and the northern United States
  • Average Weight: 97 pounds(male), 82 pounds(female)

The timberwolf(or gray wolf) has a variation of markings (gray, white, black, and brown) that act as camouflage for their environment. Wolves are very social animals, and follow a complex hierarchical pack structure, in which an alpha male and female are the only ones allowed to mate.
